Social Engineering

Phishing

Phishing is a scam tactic used to steal information and credentials from unsuspecting victims
through various contact channels such as through email or phone calls and texts.

There are various types of phishing that depict the context and factors involved in the tactic:

Spear Phishing and Whaling are examples of targeted phishing methods
while Smishing and Vishing relates to the attack vector used to approach the target.
